## Transitional tsconfig overrides (documented)

Both tsconfigs layer `noUncheckedIndexedAccess: false` + `noImplicitOverride: false` to keep adoption scope strictly config-only. Pre-adoption baseline was 0 errors in both trees. Post-adoption without overrides surfaced:
- Extension: 12 new `possibly undefined` errors across `shared/assets/*`, `src/PixelAgentsViewProvider.ts`, `src/agentManager.ts`, `src/assetLoader.ts`
- Webview: 108 new errors concentrated in `src/office/sprites/spriteData.ts` (character palette lookups) and `src/office/wallTiles.ts` (bitmask neighbor lookups)

Phase 8 will remove overrides and fix inline. Tracked in `deferred-items.md`.
